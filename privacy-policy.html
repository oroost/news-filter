Word Swapper – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

No data collection

Word Swapper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

Local storage only

Your custom rules and settings are saved using Chrome's built-in sync storage, which is managed by Google and stays within your own Chrome profile. No data is sent to any external server.

No tracking

Word Swapper contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party scripts of any kind.

Permissions

The extension uses the storage permission solely to save and sync your personal word-swap rules across your devices.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on github.com/oroost/news-filter.
